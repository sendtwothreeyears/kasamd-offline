# Strabismus Visit

**Category:** Ophthalmology
**Source:** https://www.soapnote.org/eye/strabismus-visit/

---

[textarea name="variable_1" fillable="true"]Subjective: squinting, decreased visual acuity, face turning[/textarea]
[textarea name="variable_2" fillable="true"]Objective: ***esotropia (inward eyes), exotropia (outward), hypertropia (up), hypotropia (down)***
Unequal light reflex[/textarea]
[textarea name="variable_3" fillable="true"]Objective: ***Refer to opthalmalogy if fixed or continuous at >6mo***
***Immediate referral for hypertropia or hypotropia.***
***Anticipate patching, orthoptic exercises or surgical correction.***[/textarea]
