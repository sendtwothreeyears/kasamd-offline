-rec’d updating vaccinations: live vaccines (varicella; measle, mumps, rubella) should be administered at least one month prior to pregnancy; COVID-19 vaccination is recommended
-screen for STIs: HIV, G/C, syphilis, Hepatitis B & C 
-rubella immunity [] 
-varicella immunity [] 
-genetic carrier screening
-HgbA1c [] 
-counseled on folic acid supplementation	
-abstinence from alcohol, nicotine, THC and illicit drugs encouraged 
-achieve and maintain a healthy BMI 18-24.9
-Behavioral changes to reduce the risk of acquiring infections, such as toxoplasmosis, cytomegalovirus, and listeriosis; to achieve 150 min/week or more of moderate-to-vigorous physical activity; and consume a healthy diet
