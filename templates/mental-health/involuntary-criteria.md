# Involuntary criteria

**Category:** Psychiatry
**Source:** https://www.soapnote.org/mental-health/involuntary-criteria/

---

[ ] displays acute psychiatric condition associated with a relatively sudden onset and a short severe course.
 [ ] displays a marked exacerbation of symptoms associated with a more persistent, recurring psychiatric disorder.
 [ ] poses significant danger to self or others.
 [ ] displays severe psychosocial disfunction.
