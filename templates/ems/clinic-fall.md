# Checklist for patient who fell in the waiting room of the clinic

**Category:** * Unknown
**Source:** https://www.soapnote.org/ems/clinic-fall/

---

Checklist for someone who fell in the waiting room of the clinic
[checklist name="variable_1" value="Loss of consciousness - unlikely/suspected/observed|Trauma - evaluate for injuries, c-spine precautions if needed|Stroke - headache, vision change, smile, raise arms, repeat a simple sentence (and other appropriate history/exam)|Seizure - check for injuries, postictal state, associated injuries|Hypoglycemia - check a fingerstick glucose, provide juice if needed|Arrhythmia - check an EKG, send home with a heart monitor?|Orthostasis - get orthostatic vital signs, hydration status, characterize recent intake, oral or IV hydration|Medications - interactions, missed/extra/wrong doses, address polypharmacy if possible|Generalized weakness - Elicit information from patient or caregiver regarding recent falls at home, provide wheelchair if needed|Vertigo - rotational component history, nystagmus|Get history from witnesses if time permits|Look in the chart if it's available"]
