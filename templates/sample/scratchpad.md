# 3.d. Scratchpad

**Category:** Samples, Snippets & Training
**Source:** https://www.soapnote.org/sample/scratchpad/

---

[comment memo="Scratchpad" memo_size="large" memo_style="bold"]

[link url="//soapnote.org/sample/my-favorites/" memo="(«) Previous"]

You can save the output from any SOAPnote to the Scratchpad. It has a large, but limited amount of space to store text. It includes the URL for the web page where the text was generated as well as the date and time it was generated. Once 30 kB of date has been exceeded older work will be lost, so it is important to save your work elsewhere (your EHR or some other safe location).

You must be logged in to use the Scratchpad.

To add text to the Scratchpad, every SOAPnote has a button below the output box. If you scroll down to the bottom of this page, you'll see it is the "Add to Scratchpad" button and it is to the right of the "Copy to Clipboard" button.

Your Scratchpad page is at [link url="//www.soapnote.org/scratchpad/" memo="www.soapnote.org/scratchpad"]. At this page, you can save (as a text file), copy, and delete the text in the Scratchpad.
