#1 ***Eczema/Atopic dermatitis***   
Reviewed skin care. Encourage unscented, nonirritating lubricants. Avoid irritants/perfume products. One percent hydrocortisone three times daily for redness. Return to clinic signs and symptoms reviewed. Side effects of medication discussed.
